About Zenith Computer Institute

Zenith Computer Institute, founded by Anu Sharma, is dedicated to empowering students with the latest digital skills and knowledge. Our mission is to make every student tech-savvy and confident in the digital world. We offer a wide range of courses including Typing (Hindi & English), Basic Computer Education, Business Accounting, and Web Development.

Our classrooms are equipped with modern technology, and our instructors are trained to provide practical, hands-on experience. We focus on both conceptual clarity and industry-relevant skills. Whether you are a beginner or looking to upskill, Zenith is the place to start your journey into the digital era.
